Who is Jorcell Hortelano?

Jorcell Hortelano is a Mexican independent artist and music producer born on August 2, 2006. His sound blends alternative pop with dreamy, ambient, and experimental elements. Through emotional melodies and cosmic themes, he creates a unique sonic universe rooted in vulnerability and introspection.
